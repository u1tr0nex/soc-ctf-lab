SOC Analyst CTF Lab

Ten hands-on SOC scenarios. Each challenge has 5 tasks (20 points each). Reach 100 points to unlock that scenario's flag. Progress is saved in your browser.

How it works

  • Open a challenge, read the scenario briefing, investigate the simulated logs/UI.
  • Answer each task and click Check. Wrong answers do not reveal the solution.
  • Change an answer and check again: a previously correct task can lose 20 points if now wrong.
  • Hint (-5) on a task costs 5 points from your score (you need at least 5 points first).
  • Reset challenge clears that page only and removes its saved progress.
  • Finish all 5 tasks to unlock the flag and earn a checkmark on the hub (score can be 95/100 if you used hints).
  • Complete all 10 scenarios for the SOC Analyst Lab Complete certificate below.
